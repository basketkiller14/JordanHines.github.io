I Was Reincarnated as the 7th Prince

top 20

Rating:

8.7

Comedy, Adventure, Isekai

This anime was surprisingly good and refreshing. This anime provided awesome animation along with an interesting story. This anime follows the main character Llyod through his journey of reincarnation. In Lloyd's former life he was a commoner with an insatiable thirst to learn all there is about magic. However due to Llyod being a commoner he did not possess the mana for magic. However this all changed when he was reborn Lloyd was reborn as the seventh prince. In his new body it seems he possesses a boundless amount of mana. As we follow his character throughout the story we see that Lloyd seems to be a bit of a masochist. Wherein there are no lengths he wouldn't go through to learn more about magic. We see this show cased in a couple different scenes where Lloyd comes into contact with near death experiences while continuing to learn more about magic. Overall I feel this anime is very deserving of its rating due to the masochistic comedy and the epic fight scenes that this anime provides along with a good story.
